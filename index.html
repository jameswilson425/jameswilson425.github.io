frontporch

At the end of the cohort I completed my capstone project, frontporch, where bluegrass musicians can connect for gigs and meetups. Users can create accounts and make posts to find groups to join, as well as posts for groups looking for specific musicians. It can be used for casual jam sessions and hangouts, or it can help working musicians needing a network of talent.
